🦁 7-Day Safari Adventure

📅 7 Days / 6 Nights 👥 2–12 Travelers ⭐ Best Seller 🗺️ Arusha → Serengeti → Zanzibar
01
Arrival in Arusha — Gateway to Safari
📍 Kilimanjaro International Airport → Arusha
▼
✈️ Airport Transfer 🏨 Boutique Lodge 🍽️ Welcome Dinner

Your Tanzanian adventure begins! Our representative meets you at Kilimanjaro International Airport and transfers you to your Arusha lodge. After settling in, enjoy a briefing with your safari guide over a traditional welcome dinner featuring Tanzanian cuisine. Arusha, set at the foot of Mount Meru, offers a vibrant blend of cultures as the commercial hub of northern Tanzania's safari circuit.

🌟 Highlight: Welcome dinner with your expert guide — learn about the wildlife you'll encounter
02
Into the Serengeti — First Game Drive
📍 Arusha → Serengeti National Park
▼
🚙 4x4 Safari 🦁 Game Drive 🏕️ Luxury Camp 🍳 All Meals

After an early breakfast, depart for the Serengeti — one of the world's greatest wildlife reserves. Enter through the iconic Naabi Hill Gate and begin your first game drive across the endless plains. Spot lions lounging in acacia shade, cheetahs scanning open grass, and vast wildebeest herds during the Great Migration season. Settle into your luxury tented camp as the African sun sets the savanna ablaze.

🦁 Highlight: First lion sighting on the open Serengeti plains
03
Full-Day Serengeti — Big Five Quest
📍 Serengeti National Park (Full Day)
▼
🌅 Dawn Drive 🐘 Big Five 🧺 Bush Lunch 🌙 Night Drive

An entire day in the Serengeti's heart. Rise before dawn for the magical golden hour when predators are most active. Track leopards in fever tree groves, observe elephant families at waterholes, and witness the raw drama of nature unfolding. A gourmet bush picnic lunch gives you time to reflect as zebras graze nearby. The evening offers an optional night game drive to encounter nocturnal species.

🐆 Highlight: Optional hot air balloon safari at sunrise (additional cost)
04
Ngorongoro Crater — World's Eden
📍 Serengeti → Ngorongoro Conservation Area
▼
🌋 Crater Descent 🦏 Big Five 🦩 Flamingos 🏔️ Crater Lodge

Drive to the Ngorongoro Conservation Area and descend 600 metres into the ancient crater floor — a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of Africa's most extraordinary ecosystems. Over 25,000 large animals are packed into this 260 km² caldera. Search for the rare black rhino, observe pink flamingos at Lake Magadi, and watch lion prides work the short grass plains. Overnight at a lodge perched on the crater rim.

🦏 Highlight: Encounter the critically endangered black rhino — one of Africa's rarest sights
05–07
Zanzibar Beach Relaxation
📍 Arusha → Zanzibar (via short flight)
▼
✈️ Short Flight 🏖️ Beach Resort 🌊 Snorkeling 🏛️ Stone Town Tour

Fly to Zanzibar — the Spice Island of the Indian Ocean. Three days to unwind at your beachfront resort along Nungwi's crystal shores. Explore Stone Town's UNESCO-listed labyrinthine alleys, visit a traditional spice farm, snorkel above vibrant coral reefs, and feast on Zanzibar's famous seafood. A sunset dhow cruise on Day 6 offers the perfect finale before your departure on Day 7.

🌊 Highlight: Sunset dhow cruise with fresh seafood and Swahili music
