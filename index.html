Sample Itineraries

3-Day Serengeti Experience

A quick but fulfilling dive into the vast plains of the Serengeti, witnessing the greatest wildlife spectacles on earth.

  • Day 1: Drive from Arusha to Serengeti. Afternoon game drive.
  • Day 2: Full day exploring the Serengeti plains.
  • Day 3: Morning game drive and return to base.

5-Day Ngorongoro & Tarangire

Explore the elephant-rich landscapes of Tarangire and the breathtaking wonder of the Ngorongoro Crater.

  • Day 1: Arrival and transfer to Tarangire.
  • Day 2: Full day Tarangire National Park.
  • Day 3: Transit to Ngorongoro Conservation Area.
  • Day 4: Ngorongoro Crater descent.
  • Day 5: Return to Dar es salaam.

Southern Circuit Getaway

Departing directly from Dar es salaam, experience the untouched wilderness of Nyerere National Park (Selous).

  • Day 1: Drive from Dar to Nyerere NP. Boat safari.
  • Day 2: Full day walking and driving safari.
  • Day 3: Morning game drive and return to Dar es salaam.
