Our Top Safari Packages

3-Day Serengeti Experience

A quick but fulfilling dive into the vast plains of the Serengeti, witnessing the greatest wildlife spectacles on earth.

  • Day 1: Arusha to Serengeti Drive to Serengeti. Afternoon game drive. Dinner and overnight at camp.
  • Day 2: Full Day Serengeti Exploring the endless plains tracking big cats and the migration.
  • Day 3: Serengeti to Arusha Morning game drive, followed by a scenic drive back to base.

5-Day Tarangire & Ngorongoro

Explore the elephant-rich landscapes of Tarangire and the breathtaking wonder of the Ngorongoro Crater.

  • Day 1: Arrival Transfer from airport to Tarangire lodge.
  • Day 2: Tarangire National Park Full day game drive famous for giant baobab trees and elephants.
  • Day 3: Transit Drive to Ngorongoro Conservation Area. Rest at crater rim.
  • Day 4: Ngorongoro Crater Full day descent into the crater to spot the Big Five.
  • Day 5: Departure Morning relaxation and return to Dar es salaam.

4-Day Nyerere (Selous) Circuit

Departing directly from Dar es salaam, experience the untouched wilderness of Nyerere National Park.

  • Day 1: Dar es salaam to Nyerere Drive from Dar. Evening sunset boat safari on Rufiji River.
  • Day 2: Full Day Game Drive Extensive exploration of the largest reserve in Africa.
  • Day 3: Walking Safari Guided morning walking safari. Afternoon game drive.
  • Day 4: Return Final morning view and drive back to Dar es salaam.

2-Day Mikumi Getaway

The perfect quick escape from Dar es salaam to witness lions, giraffes, zebras, and elephants over the weekend.

  • Day 1: Travel & Evening Safari Early morning drive from Dar. Arrive for a late afternoon game drive.
  • Day 2: Morning Safari & Return Sunrise game drive, breakfast, and transit back to Dar es salaam.

8-Day Bush & Beach Combo

Combine the thrill of the Tanzanian wildlife safari with the relaxing white sandy beaches of Zanzibar.

  • Days 1-4: Northern Safari Exploring Tarangire, Ngorongoro, and Serengeti.
  • Day 5: Flight to Zanzibar Transfer from bush strip to Zanzibar island.
  • Days 6-7: Beach Relaxation Enjoying the beaches, Stone Town tour, and spice farms.
  • Day 8: Departure Transfer to airport for flight home.

6-Day Kilimanjaro Trek

Conquer the roof of Africa via the beautiful Machame route. An adventure of a lifetime.

  • Day 1: Machame Gate to Machame Camp Trek through dense rainforest.
  • Day 2: Machame to Shira Camp Moorland zone hike with great views.
  • Day 3: Shira to Barranco Camp Acclimatization day passing the Lava Tower.
  • Day 4: Barranco to Barafu Camp Climbing the Barranco Wall and reaching base camp.
  • Day 5: Summit Day! Midnight trek to Uhuru Peak, descend to Mweka Camp.
  • Day 6: Mweka to Gate Final descent and certification.
